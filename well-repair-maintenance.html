Quick answer

What are the signs my well needs service?

Low or fluctuating water pressure, dirty or discolored water, air sputtering from the faucet, unusual noises, a pump that runs constantly, or a higher electric bill can all point to a well or pump problem. Sharpe's diagnoses and repairs wells across Columbia, Gaston, Lexington, and the surrounding Midlands.

When to call

Signs your well needs service

  • Low or fluctuating water pressure
  • Sputtering or air in the lines
  • Dirty, cloudy, or odd-tasting water
  • Pump running constantly or short-cycling
  • Unusual noises from the pump or pressure tank
  • Higher electric bills from the pump
  • No water at all
