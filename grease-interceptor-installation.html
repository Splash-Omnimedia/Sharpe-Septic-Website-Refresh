The right interceptor for your kitchen

An undersized or poorly installed interceptor causes problems fast. We assess your kitchen and install a system that fits how you operate.

  • Installation for new builds and upgrades

Quick answer

Do you install grease interceptors for new and existing kitchens?

Yes. Sharpe's installs properly sized grease interceptors — typically 1,000 to 3,000 gallons — for new builds and existing commercial kitchens, and coordinates the connection and final inspection. We serve commercial kitchens across the Midlands.
