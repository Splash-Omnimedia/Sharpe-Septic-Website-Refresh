Quick answer

What are the signs my septic system needs repair?

Common warning signs include backups, sewage odors, gurgling drains, slow drains, and wet or soggy areas over the drain field. You don't have to diagnose the problem yourself — describe what you're seeing and Sharpe's will determine whether a targeted repair or a full replacement is the right next step.

When to call

Signs you may need a repair or replacement

  • Frequent or recurring backups
  • Persistent sewage odors
  • Soggy ground or pooling over the field
  • Slow drains throughout the house
  • An aging system past its expected life
  • Failed inspection findings

Questions & Answers

Frequently asked questions

How do I know if I need a repair or a full replacement?

We diagnose the system first. If a repair will reliably solve the issue, that's what we recommend. If the system is failing overall, we'll explain why replacement is the better value.

Can a repair be done quickly?

Many repairs can be handled promptly. Call us and describe what's happening so we can prioritize.

What causes septic systems to fail?

Common causes include age, lack of pumping, excess water, and root intrusion. An inspection helps pinpoint the cause.
